# 🤖 AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes **10,345 AI job postings** across multiple countries, industries, and AI job roles to uncover salary trends, hiring demand, required technical skills, and recruitment patterns.

This project demonstrates how raw recruitment data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using modern data visualization techniques.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to answer important business questions such as:

* Which AI job roles offer the highest salaries?
* Which technical skills are most valuable?
* Which industries recruit the most AI professionals?
* How do education and experience influence salary?
* Which countries have the highest AI hiring demand?
* What are the latest recruitment trends in the AI industry?

# 📈 Key Insights

* AI Engineers and Machine Learning Engineers receive the highest average salaries.
* Deep Learning and Machine Learning skills have the strongest impact on salary.
* Senior professionals earn significantly higher salaries than Entry-level employees.
* Technology and Healthcare industries show the highest hiring demand.
* High hiring urgency positions generally offer better salaries.
* Practical experience has a greater influence on salary than education level.
